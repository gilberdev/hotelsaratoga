hotel saratoga habana

a rich and colorfull heritage close to the delights of old havana

Welcome to my site dedicated to the famous and hitorical Hotel Saratoga in Habana. I am “poco a poco” researching and updating it with the aim to provide as complete as possible record of the hotels ancient and recent history; bearing in mind that the goings-on today may one day be of historical interest.

My site is NOT the official website of the current hotel operator, and by no means endorsed by them; but I am grateful for their help with the research and supplying some of the photographs. You can make from this room reservations through the Bizloco eTravel System operated by Cubaism Ltd which has a direct contact with the current hotel operator.

Nigel Alexander Hunt

{# History #}

history

Toward 1933 on Prado street intersection with Dragones street was located Saratoga Hotel, it left behind its former location on Monte street, it stood the building where from then on is “Isla de Cuba Hotel”. The “new” building on Prado street was built by the Spaniard merchant Gregorio Palacios between 1879 and 1880 with the objective of lodging dwelling- houses, stores and boarding stores.
